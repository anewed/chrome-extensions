ShortsRecall Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Your privacy is our highest priority at ShortsRecall. We built this extension specifically to act as a local Second Brain.

1. Data Collection and Storage. ShortsRecall extracts metadata (video title, channel name, thumbnail URL) and spoken transcripts/captions only from videos you actively watch on youtube.com/shorts/ and tiktok.com/. All data extracted by ShortsRecall is stored locally on your device using Chrome's IndexedDB.

2. Data Transmission. We do not transmit your video history, search queries, or transcripts to any external servers, third-party databases, or analytics providers. Your data never leaves your computer.

3. Pro Version AI Capabilities. If you subscribe to ShortsRecall Pro, the AI Semantic Search feature utilizes a lightweight, local AI model (run in your browser). Your search queries and transcripts are processed entirely locally. They are not sent to OpenAI, Google, or any other external API.

4. External Services. The only external connection ShortsRecall makes is to ExtensionPay (Stripe) solely to verify your subscription status (Free vs. Pro tier) securely.

5. User Control. Because all data is stored locally, you have complete control. Uninstalling the ShortsRecall Chrome extension will permanently delete all your saved video transcripts and data from your browser.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact anewed@gmail.com.
